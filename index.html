Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to our design. Fuji Chat&Play is built on the principle of Data Sovereignty: your information belongs to you and stays on your device.

1. Data Processing Architecture

  • Local Storage: All user-generated data, including XP, pilot rank, credits, and learning history, is stored exclusively on your device via SwiftData. We do not maintain remote databases of your personal progress.
  • On-Device AI: Conversational interactions with 'Fuji,Sakura' and other characters are powered by Apple Intelligence (FoundationModels). All inference occurs locally on your iPhone's Neural Engine. No transcript of your chat is ever uploaded to a server.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, sell, or rent:

  • Biometric data or Voice recordings.
  • Contact information or Location data.
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data is stored locally, deleting the Fuji Chat&Play app will permanently remove all progress and history. This process is irreversible as we do not maintain backups of your local data.

Terms of Service

1. Scope of Service

Fuji Chat&Play provides a gamified environment for Japanese language learning. Content generated by AI characters is intended for educational simulation and should not be used as a primary source for critical translations.

2. Hardware & Performance

User acknowledges that the AI Chat Sector is technically limited to devices supporting Apple Intelligence (iPhone 15 Pro and newer). Use of the app on unsupported hardware may result in restricted functionality of specific features.

3. In-App Economy (Credits & XP)

Credits and XP earned within the app are non-monetary virtual items. They have no real-world value, cannot be transferred between users, and cannot be redeemed for legal tender.
