Privacy Policy プライバシーポリシー

Our Core Principle: Data Sovereignty

Your information belongs to you and stays on your device. We do not operate remote databases of user data. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to any party.

⚠ AI Processing Disclosure — Required by App Store Guidelines 5.1.1(i) & 5.1.2(i)

On-Device AI — Apple Intelligence

  • What AI is used: The app's AI Chat Sector uses Apple's on-device FoundationModels framework (Apple Intelligence). This is not a third-party cloud AI service.
  • What data is processed: Your typed chat messages are processed locally on your device's Neural Engine to generate character responses.
  • Where data goes: Inference runs entirely on-device. No chat transcripts, messages, or personal data are transmitted to Apple servers, our servers, or any third-party AI service.
  • Your consent: By using the AI Chat Sector, you acknowledge that your input text is processed locally by Apple Intelligence. You may avoid using this feature entirely with no loss of core learning functionality.
  • No third-party AI service is used. If Apple changes how FoundationModels processes data, Apple's own privacy policy governs that interaction.

1. Data We Store Locally on Your Device

  • Learning progress (XP, pilot rank, lesson completion)
  • In-app credits and economy state
  • App preferences and settings

All of the above is stored exclusively via SwiftData on your device. We cannot access this data remotely.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Biometric data or voice recordings
  • Chat transcripts or message history

3. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We do not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting SDK. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and users notified within the app before any data collection begins.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Because all data is local, deleting the app from your device permanently removes all associated data. This is irreversible as we hold no server-side backup.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from any user regardless of age, as no data leaves the device.

6. Regulatory Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with the App Store Review Guidelines, GDPR principles of data minimization and purpose limitation, and CCPA consumer privacy rights. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, most regulatory obligations do not apply — but we commit to the spirit of these frameworks.

7. Contact

For any privacy questions, email: covieworganization.fujichat@proton.me
